2026.03.18
Underground Layer — Deployment Progress
UPDATE
A new map layer has been added to the world system. The Underground spans 21 areas locked behind level gates starting at Level 35. Rare weapon requirements apply. Scouts report hostile entities of significantly higher threat ratings. Deploy teams at your own discretion. The layer will open server-side once the boss gate at Crownfall Citadel is cleared.
2026.03.05
Adventure Rank System Deployed
NEW
The Rank system is now live. Ranks range from F1 (Wanderer) to SSS (The Eternal). Each rank requires a combination of combat wins, exploration count, jobs completed, and rare drops. Use .rank to view your current standing and .rankup to advance when requirements are met.
2026.02.20
World Map System — 3-Layer Navigation
SYSTEM
The interactive world map now supports three distinct layers: Surface, Underground, and Aboveground. Each layer is unlocked by reaching specific level thresholds. Travel between areas uses a step-based timer system — 2 minutes per step distance. Open with .map.
2026.02.01
Economy Overhaul — Coin Caps & Shard Conversion
UPDATE
The economy has been restructured. Coin holdings are now capped by player level to prevent inflation. Shards (Cs) can be converted to Coins at a rate of 1:1000. The Work system now tracks job history for rank requirements. Daily coin cap increases with every 5 levels.
2026.01.15
Combat System v2 — Crit, Dodge, Weapon Bonuses
UPDATE
Combat has been expanded. Players now have crit chance (scales with level, capped at 35%), dodge chance (capped at 20%), and weapon damage bonuses. Combat simulation runs up to 30 turns. Winners receive 200 C + 1 Cs. Weapon drop chance: 15% per fight win. Cooldown: 5 minutes per fight.
