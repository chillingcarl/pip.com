COMMAND GUIDE
All Pip commands — click any card to expand

PROFILE
.stats
aliases: .me .profile .xp .card
+
1Displays your full player card as a visual image.
2Shows HP, ATK, DEF, crit%, dodge%, equipped weapon, and current rank.
3Stamina, status, and combat statistics are displayed on the card.
4Tag another user to view their card instead: .stats @user
.stats  |  .stats @user
.rank
aliases: .ar .adventurerank
+
1Shows your current Adventure Rank (F1 through SSS).
2Displays progress toward next rank: wins, explores, jobs, rare drops needed.
3Ranks are separate from levels — they represent reputation and grind.
4Tag another user to check their rank: .rank @user
.rank  |  .rank @user
.rankup
no aliases
+
1Attempts to advance your adventure rank to the next tier.
2Will fail with a reason if any requirement is not met.
3Requirements: min level, combat wins, explorations, jobs completed, rare drops.
4Each rank has a unique title — Wanderer, Explorer, Slayer, Worldbreaker…
.rankup
.journey
no aliases
+
1Displays your full level progression roadmap as a visual card.
2Shows all role tier milestones and which ones you've unlocked.
3XP required per level scales exponentially (formula: 100 × n²).
4Tag a user to view their journey instead: .journey @user
.journey  |  .journey @user
.userinfo
aliases: .user .who
+
1Shows a detailed player information card with all stored data.
2Includes join date, level, XP, combat record, current area, and rank.
3Coin and shard balances are also displayed on this card.
4Works on yourself by default. Tag any user to inspect their info.
.userinfo  |  .userinfo @user
.top
aliases: .lb .leaderboard
+
1Shows the top 10 players in the server ranked by XP.
2Displays each player's name, level, and total XP on a visual card.
3Leaderboard is server-specific — different servers have separate rankings.
4Earn XP by chatting in the server — meaningful messages only.
.top
ECONOMY
.balanceC
aliases: .coinbal
+
1Displays your current Coin (C) balance as a visual card.
2Coin cap scales with level — higher level = higher cap.
3Coins are earned via work, beg, combat wins, trivia, and dice.
4Check another player's balance by tagging them.
.balanceC  |  .balanceC @user
.balanceCs
aliases: .shardbal
+
1Displays your current Shard (Cs) balance.
2Shards are rarer than coins and earned through combat wins and drops.
3Convert shards to coins at a 1:1000 ratio using .convert.
4Some weapons in the hierarchy tier require Cs to purchase.
.balanceCs  |  .balanceCs @user
.work
no aliases
+
1Brings up a job selection menu with up to 10 available jobs.
2Jobs unlock at different levels — higher level jobs pay significantly more.
3Select a job by typing its number (1–10) within 30 seconds.
4Work counts toward adventure rank requirements.
.work
⏱ COOLDOWN: 1 HOUR
.beg
no aliases
+
1Begs for coins — outcome is random and area-dependent.
2In the Kingdom, you might charm a noble. In the Cave, a bat might mug you.
3Some outcomes result in coin loss — risk is part of the mechanic.
4Rare outcomes can yield bonus Shards depending on your area.
.beg
.convert
no aliases
+
1Converts Shards (Cs) to Coins (C) at a 1:1000 rate.
2Specify the number of shards to convert — all are processed at once.
3Coin cap still applies — excess coins beyond your cap are lost.
4A cap warning image is shown if you hit your coin limit.
.convert [Cs amount]
.trade
aliases: .send .tip .transfer
+
1Transfers coins to another player in the same server.
2Trade fails if you don't have enough coins in your balance.
3You cannot trade with yourself or with bots.
4Recipient receives the full amount — no tax applies.
.trade [amount] @user
COMBAT
.fight
no aliases
+
1Initiates a 1v1 PvP battle against a tagged player.
2Combat factors in HP, ATK, DEF, crit%, dodge%, and equipped weapon.
3Winner gets +200 C, +1 Cs, and a 15% chance to receive a weapon drop.
4Fight result is shown as an animated GIF with a combat log.
.fight @user
⏱ COOLDOWN: 5 MINUTES
.inventory
aliases: .inv
+
1Shows all weapons you own as a visual inventory card.
2Weapons are automatically equipped — highest damage is used in combat.
3Items are sorted by acquisition time, newest first.
4Tag another user to view their inventory: .inventory @user
.inventory  |  .inventory @user
.inf
no aliases
+
1Inspects any weapon or world entity by name.
2For weapons: shows damage, rarity, price, and how to acquire.
3For entities: shows HP, ATK, XP reward, coin reward, and their area.
4Name must match exactly — use the name shown in .blacksmith or .map.
.inf [weapon name]  |  .inf [entity name]
.blacksmith
aliases: .bs .armoury
+
1Opens the weapon shop — up to 12 weapons displayed at once.
2Weapons range from Common to Celestial rarity, each with unique stats.
3Some weapons require Coins (C), others require Shards (Cs).
4Highest-tier weapons are drop-only and cannot be purchased.
.blacksmith
.buy
no aliases
+
1Purchases a weapon from the shop by exact name.
2Will fail if the weapon is drop-only, or if you lack the required currency.
3Already-owned weapons cannot be purchased again.
4Weapon names are case-insensitive. Use the name shown in .blacksmith.
.buy [weapon name]
WORLD
.map
aliases: .worldmap .wmap
+
1Opens the interactive 3-layer world map with navigation buttons.
2Navigate between areas using ← and → buttons, then press X to travel.
3Three map tabs: SURFACE (always unlocked), UNDERGROUND (lv35+), ABOVEGROUND (lv30+).
4Travel time is based on step distance: 2 minutes per step between areas.
.map
.serverinfo
aliases: .server .si
+
1Displays a server overview card with general information.
2Shows member count, bot count, channel count, and role count.
3Server creation date and owner are displayed on the card.
4Output is a visual card generated in real time using your server icon.
.serverinfo
.roles
no aliases
+
1Shows all server role tiers and the XP/level needed to unlock each.
2Roles are automatically assigned and removed when you level up.
3Starts at "Just Spawned" and climbs to "Chronically Online" at level 16.
4Role names must match the server exactly — configured by the admin.
.roles
MINI-GAMES
.trivia
no aliases
+
1Fetches a random trivia question from Open Trivia DB.
2Four multiple-choice options are displayed (A, B, C, D). Type your answer.
3Correct answer earns +200 C. Wrong or timed out = no reward.
4You have 15 seconds to answer before the question expires.
.trivia
⏱ COOLDOWN: 20 SECONDS
.dice
aliases: .roll
+
1Rolls a die with weighted random outcomes — win or lose coins.
2Best outcome: 4-dot roll = +1000 C (35% chance). Worst: -1000 C (20%).
3Losses are capped to your actual balance — you cannot go negative.
4Result is shown as a visual dice card with the outcome and change.
.dice
⏱ COOLDOWN: 10 MINUTES
.coinflip
aliases: .flip .coin
+
1Bets a coin amount on a 50/50 heads or tails outcome.
2Win: your bet is doubled. Lose: your bet is deducted from your balance.
3Bet must not exceed your current coin balance.
4Result is shown as an animated visual card with win/loss outcome.
.coinflip [amount]
⏱ COOLDOWN: 1 MINUTE

.pip
